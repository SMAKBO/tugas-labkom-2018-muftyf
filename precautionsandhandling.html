Precaution and Handling

What to know before using cortisone? some health conditions may interact with cortisone. Tell your doctor or pharmacist if you have certain health conditions, specifically:

  • if you are pregnant, planning a pregnancy, or breastfeeding
  • if you use prescription or non-prescription drugs, herbs, or
  • if you have allergies to drugs, food, or other substances
  • if you are scheduled to vaccinate with live virus vaccines (eg, smallpox).
dietary supplements Is cortisone safe for pregnant and lactating women? There is no adequate information about the safety of using this drug during pregnancy and lactation. Always consult your doctor to consider potential benefits and risks before using this medication.
